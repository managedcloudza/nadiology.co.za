Rune reading - R500

Runes began as an ancient alphabet for Germanic and Norse peoples, evolving from the Elder Futhark of 150 AD to the Younger Futhark of the Viking Age. Laden with symbolic meanings - they carried both practical and magical significance. Rune readings, while inspired by this rich history, are a modern practice, popularized in the 20th century as a form of divination and introspection. Though their origins as a divinatory tool are less ancient than the runes themselves, they draw on the enduring mystique of these powerful symbols, captivating people worldwide to this day.
