About Me

 
Originally hailing from Russia, I have called Cape Town, South Africa, my home for the past 20 years. My fascination with spirituality has been a lifelong companion, but it was nearly 25 years ago that I truly embarked on my astrological journey. Out of curiosity, I visited an astrologist who used a distinctive system blending Western and Chinese astrology. I was utterly blown away by the accuracy of her insights into my life—she even predicted my emigration a few years later, along with other remarkable details.
 
That experience ignited a passion for astrology and related spiritual fields. I dove into research and study, and when the astrologist offered a rare course on her unique system, I immersed myself fully. This knowledge transformed my worldview, revealing that life is not a series of random events but is guided by a traceable divine intelligence. It gave my life a profound new sense of meaning. In 2004, I moved to South Africa and expanded my studies, exploring Western and Vedic astrology as well as numerology. I began practicing by creating charts for family and friends, and soon, friends of friends.
 
Over time, I built a substantial "body of evidence" affirming astrology’s power and accuracy. For years, I worked in marketing and sales in the corporate world, with astrology remaining my hobby and passion. However, the incredible feedback from my clients and the deep fulfillment I found in helping others understand and navigate their lives changed everything. I came to realize that my purpose in this lifetime is to share my spiritual knowledge with others. Embracing this calling, I have now dedicated my time and life to this pursuit.

I invite you to explore my website to learn more about how astrology can illuminate your path and to discover the services I offer.
